Alexandre Gain is one of the founders of EASE - a non profit association registered in Lyon, France aiming at promoting experiential learning and the driving force behind Practi.City, a global community digital platform dedicated to experiential learning practices and theories as well as high-definition simulation environments. 
His contribution focuses on making accessible simulation tools and curating a worldwide knowledge base for practitioners who design, evaluate, and use experiential learning systems in their curriculums.

Besides he works as an freelance Entreprise Architect since 2001.
