Alexandre Gain is the founder of EASE (a non profit association registered in Lyon, France)  and the driving force behind Practi.City, a global community platform dedicated to experiential learning practices and theories as well as high-definition simulation environments. 
Alexandre has a passion for immersive training, open-source digital solutions, and situational learning methodologies.
He works as an Entreprise Architecture since 2001, his contribution focuses on making accessible simulation tools and curating a worldwide knowledge base for practitioners who design, evaluate, and use experiential learning systems in their curriculums.
