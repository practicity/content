I am a software developer with a background in Computer Science and a strong interest in simulation systems and experiential learning. I have worked with JaamSim, where I developed custom extensions to enable real-time communication through Apache Kafka. This allows simulations to both publish and consume events, bridging simulation environments with real-world data pipelines. I am particularly interested in building interactive, event-driven systems that enhance learning through realistic and dynamic experiences.
