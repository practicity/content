At Practi.City, we believe true expertise is lived, not memorized. 
While VR and digital games are great starters, the deepest breakthroughs happen in the arena of real-world, real-scale simulations. 

We are a collective radar for this learning revolution. 
Practi.City is a curation hub tracking down the boldest articles, videos, and initiatives in experiential learning from across the globe.
We spotlight the visionaries proving that the best way to learn is to step into the fire. 

Got a radar for mind-blowing educational content? Or are you out there designing real-world simulations yourself? Practi.City is a collective effort, and we want your voice. We are actively seeking curators and contributors to help fuel our blog. Join us.
